TaskFlow Sync

A simple, offline-first task manager for Android.

What it does

  • Capture tasks fast — text or voice.
  • Reliable on-device reminders that fire on time, even after a reboot.
  • Optional one-way sync to your Google Calendar (you choose which tasks).
  • Works fully offline. Your tasks never leave your device unless you turn on Calendar sync.

Privacy

TaskFlow Sync has no backend. Tasks live on your device. Google Calendar access, if you grant it, is used only to create events for tasks you sync — nothing else. Read the full privacy policy.
